Welcome to my music rating website! This site is currently only at the beginning stages of construction and is being designed and published for COMP333 - Software Engineering at Wesleyan University.

What you will be able to do on this website

Rate Songs
Log into a Personal Account
Search for ratings of your favorite songs
Check out your past reviews
Use our Mobile App!
   

1. Rate Songs

You will be able to rate your favorite (or not so favorite) songs on a scale from 0-10 out of 10, and then leave a review for others to view as to why you gave it the rating you did.

2. Log into Personal Account

You will be able to log into your own personal account using your own personally generated username and password. From this account you will be able to leave your own reviews instead of just viewing others!

3. Search for ratings of your favorite songs

You will be able to search the title of your favorite songs and bring up the reviews and ratings that have been left on it.

4. Check out your past reviews

From your personal account you will be able to view and edit your own past reviews of songs!

5. Mobile App

Our site will also eventually be available to download on Apple and Android products in the form as an app in their respective app store!
